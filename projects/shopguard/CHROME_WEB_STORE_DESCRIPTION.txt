ShopGuard — Free Shopping Protection for Every Online Shopper

Shop smarter. Never get tricked again. ShopGuard is a free Chrome extension that scans any shopping page for fake reviews, hidden fees, and manipulative dark patterns — giving you the facts before you buy.

Completely free. No account required. No paywall. Just install and shop safely.


WHY SHOPGUARD?

Online shopping is full of tricks designed to make you spend more than you should. Fake urgency timers, inflated reviews, hidden shipping fees, and deceptive pricing are everywhere. ShopGuard puts the evidence in your hands so you can make informed decisions.

If you used Fakespot before it shut down in 2025, or you're looking for a Honey alternative that actually protects you from deceptive pricing — ShopGuard is built for you.


WHAT SHOPGUARD DETECTS

1. Dark Pattern Detection (14 Types)
ShopGuard identifies 14 categories of manipulative design patterns, including:
  - Fake urgency ("Only 2 left!" timers that reset on refresh)
  - Fake social proof (fabricated "X people viewing this" counters)
  - Confirmshaming ("No thanks, I don't want to save money")
  - Hidden costs and drip pricing
  - Bait-and-switch tactics
  - Forced continuity (sneaky auto-renewals)
  - Preselected options you didn't choose
  - Misdirection and visual tricks
  - Disguised ads posing as content
  - Trick questions designed to confuse
  - Obstruction (making cancellation difficult)
  - Nagging (repeated prompts to wear you down)

Each detection includes the exact evidence found on the page and a severity rating (high, medium, low), so you can judge for yourself.

2. Fake Review Detection (7 Statistical Signals)
ShopGuard analyzes reviews using 7 evidence-based signals:
  - Date clustering: Are reviews suspiciously bunched on the same dates?
  - Rating distribution anomaly: Is the star-rating pattern unnatural?
  - Phrase repetition: Do multiple reviews use identical wording?
  - Length uniformity: Are all reviews suspiciously similar in length?
  - Incentive keywords: Do reviews mention free products or discounts for reviewing?
  - Rating surge detection: Did the product suddenly get a flood of 5-star reviews?
  - AI-generated content detection: Do reviews show signs of being written by AI?

This is not guesswork. Every finding is backed by data you can verify yourself.

3. Hidden Fee Scanner
Catches pricing tricks before you reach checkout:
  - Drip pricing (fees added one by one through the checkout flow)
  - Hidden shipping and handling charges
  - Surprise service fees and taxes not shown upfront
  - Deceptive price anchoring (fake "original" prices)

4. Price Comparison
  - Compare prices across sources with fee-inclusive totals
  - Detect pricing outliers and suspicious discounts
  - Understand the true all-in cost before you commit
