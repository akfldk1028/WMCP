BizScope AI — AI Business Strategy Report Generator

Analyze any company or business idea with AI-powered strategic frameworks. Right-click any company name to instantly generate a consulting-grade strategy report.

FEATURES:
- Company Analysis: 12 strategic frameworks (PEST, Porter's 5 Forces, SWOT, TOWS, McKinsey 7S, and more)
- Idea Validation: 8 frameworks with a 9-dimension scorecard for Go/No-Go decisions
- Multi-Model Ensemble: Cross-validation using Claude, GPT-4o, Grok, and Gemini (Pro)
- Context Menu: Right-click any text to analyze as a company or idea
- Keyboard Shortcut: Alt+Shift+B for quick access
- License Management: Enter your license key in extension settings

HOW TO USE:
1. Select any company name on a web page
2. Right-click → "BizScope AI로 기업 분석" or "아이디어 검증"
3. BizScope AI opens and automatically starts analysis
4. View your complete strategy report in 3 minutes

PRICING:
- Free: 2 reports (no sign-up required)
- Per-report: $5 per report
- Pro: $29/month (unlimited reports + ensemble AI)

This extension connects to https://bizscope-rho.vercel.app for AI analysis processing.

Supports Korean and English interfaces.
